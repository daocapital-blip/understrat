what makes this different from anything else in DeFi six advantages in a single deposit

  1. Every direction covered

    Long, short, or neutral on any ERC-20 token. Six strategies per pod. Whatever the market does, there's a position for it.

    One protocol. Every directional view.

  2. Leverage without the liquidation cliff

    An auto-pilot continuously adjusts your leverage before it reaches danger. A 2x perp gets liquidated at -50% and you lose everything. A StratsFi position at the same price drop still holds 62.5% of your capital. You survive.

    Same leverage. You stay in the trade.

  3. Returns that accelerate the more you're right

    You win more AND lose less. Your gains compound faster the further the price moves in your favour. When a token rises 400%, Strats returns +2,400% vs +800% for a 2x perp. These are payoff shapes that tradfi sells as structured products to accredited investors. Strats puts them on-chain, with no expiry, no counterparty, and real yield on top.

    More upside. Less downside. Automatically.

  4. Real yield on every position

    Lending interest + AMM trading fees + arbitrage revenue. Proven at 100-300% APY on Peapods' most volatile pods. Earnings come from real trading activity, not token emissions.

    Your position earns while it sits.

  5. Maximise your capital instead of keeping it idle

    $1 deployed controls a $4 leveraged position. On the flagship strategies (SuperETH+, SuperUSDC+), you get ~50% of your deposit back as liquid cash at entry, deployable anywhere else while your position runs.

    Your money does two jobs at once.

  6. Simple to use

    Pick a strategy, deposit, done. No manual rebalancing. No funding rates. See your exact payoff at every price before you enter.

    Powerful engine. One-click entry.
