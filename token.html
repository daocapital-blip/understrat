Act 3 · part 1 · the alignment

60% of every revenue dollar goes to holders. From day one.

Most DeFi tokens don't accrue value. Governance votes that go nowhere, fee switches that never flip, revenue that sits in treasuries holders can't touch. $STRATS is different: 60% of protocol revenue buys back $STRATS (shrinking supply and rewarding stakers). 40% funds operations and growth to keep the engine expanding. More usage, more revenue, more buybacks. That's the whole alignment.
